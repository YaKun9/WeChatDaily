# 风格：便利店货架速报

适合轻松、社交平台感强的总结。话题像货架商品标签，群友像值班店员角色卡。

```text
Use case: infographic-diagram
Asset type: vertical Chinese WeChat chat summary poster
Primary request: Generate a creative convenience-store shelf report poster for the WeChat chat "{chat_name}" from {start_date} to {end_date}. This is generated raster illustration, not HTML and not a screenshot.

Required visible text:
Title: "{chat_name}"
Report name: "{report_name}"
Date line: "{date_line} · {message_count}条消息"
Subtitle: "{subtitle}"

Section 01 title: "今日上架"
Shelf labels:
1. "{topic_1}"
2. "{topic_2}"
3. "{topic_3}"
4. "{topic_4}"
5. "{topic_5}"
6. "{topic_6}"

Section 02 title: "值班群友"
Member cards:
{member_cards}

Section 03 title: "店长小票"
Conclusion: "{conclusion}"

Visual style: cheerful illustrated convenience-store notice board, product shelf tags, receipt paper, hand-drawn stickers, marker outlines, compact information cards. Fresh, casual, and social-media friendly.
Composition: vertical 2:3 poster; store-sign title at top; shelf-topic cards in middle; member avatar cards as staff badges below; conclusion on receipt strip at bottom.
Avatar rules: use gender from each member card; no real portraits; role props can be scanner, receipt, wrench, mic, folder, radar, terminal, chart.
Palette: cream, black ink, mint green, tomato red, sunflower yellow, teal, warm orange. Avoid purple-blue gradient dominance.
Constraints: no data-overview/statistics-overview section, no raw chat logs, no phone numbers, no QR codes, no watermark, no screenshots, short readable Chinese labels only.
```
