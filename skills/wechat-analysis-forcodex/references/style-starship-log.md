# 风格：星舰航行日志

适合技术排障、工具链、Agent、模型讨论多的群。画面像复古科幻飞船任务日志，但信息结构仍是日报/周报海报。

```text
Use case: infographic-diagram
Asset type: vertical Chinese WeChat chat summary poster
Primary request: Generate a creative "starship mission log" poster for the WeChat chat "{chat_name}" from {start_date} to {end_date}. This is raster illustration, not HTML and not a screenshot.

Required visible text:
Title: "{chat_name}"
Report name: "{report_name}"
Date line: "{date_line} · {message_count}条消息"
Subtitle: "{subtitle}"

Section 01 title: "今日航线"
Topic nodes:
1. "{topic_1}"
2. "{topic_2}"
3. "{topic_3}"
4. "{topic_4}"
5. "{topic_5}"
6. "{topic_6}"

Section 02 title: "舰员角色卡"
Member cards:
{member_cards}

Section 03 title: "舰长批注"
Conclusion: "{conclusion}"

Visual style: retro sci-fi mission board, cream paper mixed with dark space panels, hand-drawn control labels, orbit lines, radar screens, tiny dashboard icons. Keep it playful and readable, not dark or cinematic.
Composition: vertical 2:3 poster; title bridge at top; route-map topic nodes in middle; 2x4 crew role cards with chibi avatars below; conclusion strip at bottom.
Avatar rules: use gender from each member card; no real portraits; role props can be terminal, radar, wrench, blueprint, microphone, shield, chart.
Palette: cream, black ink, teal, amber, coral, mint, small silver accents. Avoid purple-blue gradient dominance.
Constraints: no data-overview/statistics-overview section, no real brand logos, no watermark, no screenshots, text must be short and legible.
```
